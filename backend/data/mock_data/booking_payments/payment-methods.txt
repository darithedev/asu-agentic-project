ACCEPTED PAYMENT METHODS

We accept the following payment methods for your booking:

CREDIT AND DEBIT CARDS
- Visa (all types)
- Mastercard
- American Express
- Discover
- Diners Club

All card payments are processed securely through encrypted payment gateways. International customers may see charges in local currency converted from USD.

PAYMENT PLAN OPTIONS
For packages over $1,500, we offer payment plans:

- 50% at booking, 50% 30 days before travel
- 33% at booking, 33% at 45 days, 34% at 30 days before travel
- Custom payment plans available for packages over $3,000

Final payment is typically due 30 days before departure. Failure to pay may result in cancellation.

PAYPAL
PayPal payments accepted for full booking amounts or partial payments. PayPal account must be verified.

WIRE TRANSFERS
Wire transfers accepted for large bookings (typically $5,000+). Allow 3-5 business days for processing. Instructions provided upon request.

BANK TRANSFERS
Domestic bank transfers accepted with 3-5 business day processing time. Contact customer service for account details.

CURRENCY
All prices displayed in USD. International customers will see converted amounts based on current exchange rates. Exchange rate locked at time of booking confirmation.

SECURITY
All payment information is encrypted and stored securely. We comply with PCI DSS standards for payment card security. We never store complete credit card numbers on our servers.

REFUND PROCESSING
Refunds, when applicable per our cancellation policy, are processed to the original payment method:
- Credit/Debit cards: 5-10 business days
- PayPal: 3-5 business days
- Wire transfers: 7-14 business days

If original payment method is unavailable, we'll contact you for alternative refund method.

FEES
- No booking fees for standard payments
- International transaction fees may apply (charged by your bank, not us)
- Wire transfer fees: $25-50 (sender responsibility)
- Payment plan fees: None (standard plans)
