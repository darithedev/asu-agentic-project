FLIGHT COST REFERENCE GUIDE

Approximate flight pricing for popular routes (economy class, round-trip, per person).

DOMESTIC (USA)
- New York to Los Angeles: $300-600
- New York to Miami: $200-400
- Chicago to San Francisco: $350-650
- Boston to Las Vegas: $350-600

Prices vary significantly by:
- Booking timing (book 2-3 months ahead for best rates)
- Travel dates (weekends, holidays more expensive)
- Season (summer and holidays peak pricing)

EUROPE
- New York to Paris: $600-1,200
- New York to London: $550-1,100
- Los Angeles to Rome: $800-1,400
- Miami to Madrid: $700-1,300

Peak season (June-August): Expect prices at higher end of range.

ASIA
- Los Angeles to Tokyo: $800-1,500
- San Francisco to Bangkok: $900-1,600
- New York to Singapore: $1,100-1,800
- Chicago to Seoul: $950-1,700

CARIBBEAN AND MEXICO
- New York to Cancun: $400-800
- Miami to Jamaica: $350-700
- Atlanta to Dominican Republic: $400-750

BUSINESS CLASS PREMIUM
Generally 3-5x economy pricing:
- Domestic: +$800-2,000
- Europe: +$2,000-4,000
- Asia: +$3,000-6,000

BAGGAGE FEES
- First checked bag: $35-50 (domestic), $50-75 (international)
- Second checked bag: $45-60 (domestic), $75-100 (international)
- Overweight bags (>50 lbs): $100-200
- Oversized bags: $100-200

SEAT SELECTION
- Standard seat: Free or $0-25
- Preferred seat (extra legroom): $25-75
- Exit row: $50-150

Prices shown are approximate and fluctuate daily based on demand, fuel costs, and airline pricing strategies. Book early for best rates, especially during peak travel periods.
