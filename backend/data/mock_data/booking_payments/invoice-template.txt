INVOICE INFORMATION

Your booking invoice includes the following details and breakdown:

INVOICE HEADER
- Invoice number (unique booking reference)
- Invoice date
- Booking date
- Travel dates
- Customer name and contact information
- Booking reference number

ITEMIZED CHARGES
1. Base Package Price
   - Flight cost (per person)
   - Hotel accommodation (per room, per night)
   - Package type (economy, mid-range, premium)

2. Room/Seat Upgrades
   - Room category upgrades
   - Seat selection fees
   - Early check-in/late check-out fees

3. Add-Ons
   - Travel insurance premium
   - Airport transfers
   - Guided tours
   - Meal plans
   - Airport lounge access

4. Taxes and Fees
   - Government taxes (airport, departure taxes)
   - Hotel city taxes
   - Service fees
   - Processing fees (if applicable)

5. Discounts Applied
   - Early booking discounts
   - Group discounts
   - Promotional codes
   - Loyalty program discounts

TOTAL BREAKDOWN
- Subtotal (before taxes and fees)
- Taxes and fees total
- Discounts total
- Final total amount due

PAYMENT INFORMATION
- Amount paid to date
- Amount remaining
- Payment due date
- Payment method(s) used

TERMS AND CONDITIONS
- Cancellation policy reference
- Refund policy reference
- Terms of service acknowledgment

DOCUMENTATION
Your invoice serves as:
- Booking confirmation
- Payment receipt (when fully paid)
- Proof of purchase for travel insurance
- Reference for modifications or cancellations

Invoice is automatically generated upon booking confirmation and sent via email. Additional copies available upon request through customer portal or by contacting billing department.

All amounts in USD unless otherwise specified. International customers will see equivalent amounts in local currency.
