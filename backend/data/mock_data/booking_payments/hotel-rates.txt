HOTEL RATE GUIDE

Average nightly rates per room (USD) for various destinations and hotel categories.

PARIS, FRANCE
- Budget (2-star): $80-120 per night
- Mid-range (3-star): $150-250 per night
- Upscale (4-star): $250-400 per night
- Luxury (5-star): $400-800+ per night

Peak season (summer): +30-50%

LONDON, UK
- Budget (2-star): $100-140 per night
- Mid-range (3-star): $180-280 per night
- Upscale (4-star): $300-500 per night
- Luxury (5-star): $500-1,000+ per night

TOKYO, JAPAN
- Business hotel (3-star): $120-200 per night
- Mid-range (4-star): $200-350 per night
- Upscale (5-star): $400-800 per night

Note: Rooms are typically smaller than Western standards.

NEW YORK CITY, USA
- Budget (2-star): $150-200 per night
- Mid-range (3-star): $200-350 per night
- Upscale (4-star): $350-600 per night
- Luxury (5-star): $600-1,500+ per night

Manhattan locations command premium pricing.

BANGKOK, THAILAND
- Budget (3-star): $40-80 per night
- Mid-range (4-star): $80-150 per night
- Upscale (5-star): $150-400 per night

Excellent value destination.

CANCELLATION POLICIES
- Non-refundable: Up to 20% discount, no cancellation
- Free cancellation: Can cancel up to 24-48 hours before arrival
- Flexible: Can modify dates with advance notice

EXTRA CHARGES
- City taxes: $2-20 per night (varies by city)
- Resort fees: $20-50 per night (some destinations)
- Early check-in: $50-100 (if available)
- Late check-out: $50-100 (if available)

INCLUSIONS
Standard inclusions:
- Room accommodation
- Basic toiletries
- Daily housekeeping
- Wi-Fi (sometimes free, sometimes paid)

May include:
- Breakfast (check package details)
- Airport transfers (premium packages)
- Access to hotel amenities

All rates are approximate and vary by:
- Season and holidays
- Day of week (weekends often more expensive)
- Special events (conferences, festivals)
- Booking timing (last minute often higher)

Rates confirmed at time of booking. Prices in local currency may vary.
