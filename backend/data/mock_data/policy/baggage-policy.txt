BAGGAGE POLICY

AIRLINE BAGGAGE ALLOWANCES

Standard allowances vary by airline and fare class. General guidelines:

CARRY-ON BAGGAGE
- Typically: 1 carry-on bag + 1 personal item
- Size limits: 22" x 14" x 9" (varies by airline)
- Weight limit: 15-22 lbs (varies by airline)
- Personal item: purse, laptop bag, small backpack

CHECKED BAGGAGE
- Economy: Typically 0-1 bag included (varies by airline and route)
- Premium Economy: 1-2 bags included
- Business/First Class: 2 bags included
- Weight limit per bag: 50 lbs (23 kg) standard
- Size limit: 62 linear inches (length + width + height)

OVERWEIGHT/OVERSIZED FEES
- Overweight (50-70 lbs): $100-200 per bag
- Overweight (70+ lbs): $200-400 per bag (may be refused)
- Oversized (62-80 linear inches): $100-200 per bag
- Oversized (80+ linear inches): May be refused or charged $200-400

ADDITIONAL BAGGAGE
- First checked bag (if not included): $35-75
- Second checked bag: $45-100
- Third+ checked bag: $150-200 each

SPECIAL ITEMS
- Sports equipment (golf clubs, skis): $50-150 each
- Musical instruments: Varies, may require separate seat
- Fragile items: Special handling fees may apply

RESTRICTED ITEMS

Prohibited in carry-on or checked:
- Explosives, flammable items
- Weapons, sharp objects (in carry-on)
- Liquids over 3.4 oz (in carry-on)
- Certain batteries (check airline policy)

HOTEL BAGGAGE

Most hotels:
- Can store luggage before check-in/after check-out (free or minimal fee)
- Liability for stored luggage typically limited
- Valuables should not be left in stored luggage

LOST OR DAMAGED BAGGAGE

If baggage is lost or damaged:
1. File report with airline immediately (before leaving airport)
2. Obtain written report/reference number
3. Keep all receipts for replacement items
4. File claim with airline (deadline typically 7-21 days)
5. Contact travel insurance if you have baggage coverage

Airline liability typically limited to:
- Domestic: $3,500 per passenger
- International: Approximately $1,700 (varies by convention)

Travel insurance may provide additional coverage.

PACKING TIPS

- Pack essentials and medications in carry-on
- Label all bags with contact information
- Take photos of bag contents for insurance claims
- Don't pack valuables in checked baggage
- Check airline website for specific restrictions

SPECIAL ASSISTANCE

For travelers with mobility needs or medical equipment:
- Contact airline in advance (typically 48+ hours)
- Medical equipment typically allowed in addition to standard allowance
- Wheelchairs and mobility aids usually free
