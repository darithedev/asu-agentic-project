REFUND TERMS AND CONDITIONS

COMPLETE REFUNDS

You are eligible for a complete refund (minus processing fees) if:
1. You cancel within the full refund period (60+ days before departure)
2. We cancel your booking due to our error or inability to provide services
3. Significant changes are made to your booking that you did not approve
4. Documented medical emergency prevents travel (case-by-case review)

PARTIAL REFUNDS

Partial refunds are available based on cancellation timing (see Cancellation Policy). Refund percentages decrease as departure date approaches.

NON-REFUNDABLE ITEMS

The following are generally non-refundable once booked:
- Non-refundable airline tickets
- Processing fees ($100 per person)
- Travel insurance premiums (may be transferable)
- Certain hotel bookings marked as non-refundable

REFUND PROCESSING TIME

Once cancellation is confirmed:
- Refund processing begins within 2 business days
- Credit card refunds: 5-10 business days to appear on statement
- PayPal refunds: 3-5 business days
- Bank/wire transfers: 7-14 business days

Refund timing also depends on your financial institution's processing time.

DISPUTED CHARGES

If you believe you've been incorrectly charged:
1. Contact customer service within 30 days of charge
2. Provide booking reference and explanation
3. We'll review and respond within 10 business days
4. If valid, refund will be processed per standard timeline

CHARGEBACKS

We strongly encourage contacting us directly to resolve billing issues before initiating chargebacks. Chargebacks may result in account restrictions. We're committed to resolving all issues fairly.

REFUND METHOD

Refunds are issued to the original payment method used for booking:
- If original payment method is no longer valid, contact us for alternative arrangement
- Refunds cannot be issued to different credit cards or accounts without verification

SPECIAL PROMOTIONS

Refund terms for promotional bookings (flash sales, limited-time offers) may differ. Terms clearly stated at time of booking for promotional rates.

GROUP BOOKINGS

Refund terms for group bookings outlined in separate group agreement contract.
