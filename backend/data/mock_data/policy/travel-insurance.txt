TRAVEL INSURANCE POLICY

COVERAGE OVERVIEW

Our travel insurance plans provide comprehensive protection for your trip investment and peace of mind.

COVERED EVENTS

Trip Cancellation:
- Covered reasons include: illness, injury, death of traveler or family member, severe weather, job loss, jury duty
- Reimbursement up to trip cost

Trip Interruption:
- If you must cut your trip short due to covered events
- Reimbursement for unused, prepaid, non-refundable expenses

Trip Delay:
- Coverage if trip is delayed 6+ hours due to covered reasons
- Reimbursement for additional accommodation/meal expenses (typically $100-200 per day, up to limit)

Medical Emergency:
- Emergency medical coverage while traveling
- Medical evacuation coverage
- Coverage amounts vary by plan (typically $50,000-250,000)

Baggage Loss/Damage:
- Coverage for lost, stolen, or damaged baggage
- Typically $500-2,500 per person
- Does not cover high-value items unless declared

EXCLUSIONS

Not covered:
- Pre-existing medical conditions (unless waiver purchased)
- Known events at time of booking (hurricanes, political unrest you were aware of)
- Alcohol/drug-related incidents
- Extreme sports or dangerous activities (unless specified coverage added)
- War, terrorism (may be available as separate add-on)
- Mental health conditions (unless specified)

PLAN TYPES

Basic Plan:
- Trip cancellation/interruption: Up to trip cost
- Medical: $50,000
- Baggage: $500
- Trip delay: $100/day

Standard Plan:
- Trip cancellation/interruption: Up to trip cost
- Medical: $100,000
- Baggage: $1,000
- Trip delay: $150/day

Premium Plan:
- Trip cancellation/interruption: Up to trip cost + 150%
- Medical: $250,000
- Baggage: $2,500
- Trip delay: $200/day
- Cancel for Any Reason option available

PRICING

Insurance premiums typically range from:
- 4-8% of total trip cost for Basic/Standard
- 10-15% of total trip cost for Premium

Exact pricing calculated at time of booking based on trip details.

CLAIMS PROCESS

To file a claim:
1. Contact insurance provider immediately when incident occurs
2. Obtain all documentation (receipts, medical records, police reports)
3. Complete claim form (available online or by request)
4. Submit with supporting documentation
5. Claims typically processed within 10-30 business days

PRE-EXISTING CONDITIONS

Pre-existing conditions may be covered if:
- Insurance purchased within 14-21 days of initial trip deposit
- Waiver purchased (additional fee may apply)
- Condition stable for specified period before travel

Review policy details for specific requirements.

PURCHASE TIMING

Insurance must be purchased:
- Before final payment (typically 30 days before travel)
- Some benefits require purchase within 14-21 days of initial booking deposit

We strongly recommend purchasing insurance at time of booking for maximum coverage.
