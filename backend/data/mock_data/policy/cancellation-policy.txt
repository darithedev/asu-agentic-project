CANCELLATION POLICY

This policy outlines the terms and conditions for cancelling your travel booking.

CANCELLATION TIME FRAMES

Full Refund Eligibility:
- Cancellations made 60+ days before departure: Full refund minus $100 processing fee per person
- Cancellations made 45-59 days before departure: 75% refund
- Cancellations made 30-44 days before departure: 50% refund
- Cancellations made 15-29 days before departure: 25% refund
- Cancellations made 0-14 days before departure: No refund

EXCEPTIONS

Non-Refundable Bookings:
- Bookings marked as "Non-Refundable" or "Basic Economy" cannot be cancelled for refund
- Non-refundable bookings may be eligible for future travel credit (see below)

SPECIAL CIRCUMSTANCES

Medical Emergencies:
Documented medical emergencies preventing travel may qualify for full or partial refund. Medical documentation required. Case reviewed on individual basis.

Death in Family:
Full refund available with proper documentation (death certificate).

Natural Disasters/Travel Bans:
If destination becomes unsafe or inaccessible due to government travel bans or natural disasters, full refund or future credit offered.

HOW TO CANCEL

1. Online: Log into your account on our website, navigate to "My Bookings," select booking, click "Cancel"
2. Phone: Call 1-800-XXX-XXXX during business hours
3. Email: Send cancellation request to cancellations@travelagency.com

Cancellation is effective when we confirm receipt. Processing time: 5-10 business days for refund processing.

REFUND PROCESSING

Refunds processed to original payment method:
- Credit/Debit cards: 5-10 business days
- PayPal: 3-5 business days
- Wire transfers: 7-14 business days

FUTURE TRAVEL CREDITS

For cancellations not eligible for refund:
- Future travel credit may be issued (typically 80% of booking value)
- Credit valid for 12 months from issue date
- Can be applied to any future booking
- Credit is non-transferable

GROUP BOOKINGS

Group bookings (8+ people) may have separate cancellation terms outlined in group contract.

QUESTIONS

Contact us for cancellation requests or questions:
Email: cancellations@travelagency.com
Phone: 1-800-XXX-XXXX

This policy applies to bookings made on or after January 1, 2024. Bookings made prior to this date are subject to terms in effect at time of booking.
