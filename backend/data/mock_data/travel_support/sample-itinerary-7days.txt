7-DAY EUROPEAN ADVENTURE ITINERARY

This sample itinerary covers three major European cities in one week.

DAY 1: PARIS
- Morning: Arrival at CDG airport, check into hotel in central Paris
- Afternoon: Visit the Eiffel Tower (book tickets in advance)
- Evening: Stroll along the Seine River, dinner in a traditional bistro

DAY 2: PARIS
- Morning: Louvre Museum (opens at 9am, arrive early)
- Afternoon: Walk through Tuileries Garden, visit Place de la Concorde
- Evening: Montmartre neighborhood, dinner with view of city

DAY 3: PARIS TO AMSTERDAM
- Morning: Take high-speed train from Gare du Nord (approx 3.5 hours)
- Afternoon: Arrive Amsterdam, check in, explore Jordaan district
- Evening: Canal cruise (best views of city)

DAY 4: AMSTERDAM
- Morning: Van Gogh Museum (pre-book tickets)
- Afternoon: Anne Frank House (book months in advance) or Rijksmuseum
- Evening: Explore De Pijp neighborhood, local dining

DAY 5: AMSTERDAM TO BERLIN
- Morning: Flight from Amsterdam to Berlin (1.5 hours) or train (6 hours)
- Afternoon: Check in Berlin hotel, visit Brandenburg Gate
- Evening: Experience Berlin nightlife in Kreuzberg district

DAY 6: BERLIN
- Morning: Berlin Wall Memorial and Checkpoint Charlie
- Afternoon: Museum Island (Pergamon Museum, Berlin Cathedral)
- Evening: Traditional German dinner, sample local beers

DAY 7: BERLIN DEPARTURE
- Morning: Visit Reichstag Building (free but requires advance booking)
- Afternoon: Final shopping, head to airport for departure

TIPS FOR THIS ITINERARY
- Purchase a Eurail pass if traveling by train
- Book accommodations near city centers for easy access
- Reserve major attractions 2-4 weeks in advance
- Pack comfortable walking shoes
- Allow buffer time between activities
- Consider travel insurance for multi-city trip
